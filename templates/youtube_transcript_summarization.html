YouTube Transcript Summerization

A YouTube transcript is a written version of the audio and video content of a YouTube video. It's a text-based representation of the spoken words, sounds, and music in a video.

Transcripts can be useful for a variety of purposes.

Improving video accessibility for viewers who are deaf or hard of hearing

Enhancing video searchability and discoverability

Providing a written record of the video content for reference or quotation

Facilitating language translation and subtitles

And many other uses!
